FIFA

Game rules
BITSIAN ONLY FIFA tournament
Knockout Elimination:
The event will have single match elimination (Neutral stadium) until the Round of 16. After that, there will be a two-match Home-Away type of Elimination.

COD-MOBILE

Game rules
COD Mobile is a Traditional 5v5 team-based combat game. The game modes included in the tournament are Hardpoint, Domination, and Search and Destroy.
The tournament will be played in a knockout elimination format.
The maps included in the pool are:
  • Crossfire
  • Standoff
  • Takeoff
  • Firing Range
  • Raid
  • Crash
  • Hackney Yard

CSGO

Game rules
The tournament will be played in knockout elimination format.
  • Matches till the Round of 16: Best of 1
  • Quarters and Semis: Best of 3
  • Finals: Best of 5
The maps included in the pool are:
  • Mirage
  • Inferno
  • Dust2
  • Nuke
  • Train
  • Vertigo
  • Overpass
  • Ancient

VALORANT

Game rules
Knockout elimination format
  • Matches till Round of 16: Best of 1
  • Quarters and Semifinals: Best of 3
  • Finals: Best of 5
Map Pool: The tournament will be played on the following maps:
  • Ascent
  • Bind
  • Split
  • Haven
  • Icebox
  • Breeze
  • Fracture
