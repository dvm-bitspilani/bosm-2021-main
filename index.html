    About Us

    After more than a year-long wait and boredom due to the pandemic, all of us have been waiting to taste the campus life. Sports events are an integral part of our campus life and a way to bond among our peers. We have an exciting event lined up for you. We present BITS Premier League (BPL), a competition for everyone to showcase their talents and strive to be the best.

    Mark your calendar for a couple of engaging and refreshing weekends, 19th to 21st November and 26th to 28th November, and be prepared to get tested among your peers. The event will consist of various sports tournaments including Cricket, Ultimate Frisbee, Athletics, Football, Table Tennis and many more. The online games included are CSGO, Valorant, CODM and FIFA. The dates for the same are 21st - 24th November.

    Updated Rules


    1. All BITSians and Faculty members are welcome to participate in all sports.
    2. On-spot registrations won’t be allowed.
    3. We are extending the deadline to register for BPL to 15th November 11:59 PM. Students can register for events using the registration portal.
    4. We are adding the following events for Badminton- Badminton Boys doubles- Two boys should register together. Matches would be best of 3. Badminton Girls doubles- Two girls should register together. Matches would be best of 3.
      You may participate in both the mixed team and doubles events.
    5. BITSian Boys are now allowed to register in any two sports. For those who have already registered in any sport - You can now register in any other sport except for Cricket and Football. For those who have not registered in BPL - You can either register in Cricket or Football as your first sport and register in any other sport except for Cricket and Football as your second sport. Or, you can register in any two sports, neither of them can be Cricket/Football. The registration portal would accept registrations for the 2nd sport from 15th November 7 AM till 15th November EOD.

    Note: Any individual can participate in only a maximum of two sports. We will try to resolve all the clashes as much as possible.
