About Us

After more than a year-long wait and boredom due to the pandemic, all of us have been waiting to taste the campus life. Sports events are an integral part of our campus life and a way to bond among our peers. We have an exciting event lined up for you. We present BITS Premier League (BPL), a competition for everyone to showcase their talents and strive to be the best.

Mark your calendar for a couple of engaging and refreshing weekends, 19th to 21st November and 26th to 28th November, and be prepared to get tested among your peers. The event will consist of various sports tournaments including Cricket, Ultimate Frisbee, Athletics, Football, Table Tennis and many more. The online games included are CSGO, Valorant, CODM and FIFA. The dates for the same are 21st - 24th November.
