    ABOUT US
    The BITS Open Sports Meet (BOSM) is the annual sports festival of BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus. This much awaited sporting extravaganza, held from 13th November to 17th November, is a platform for college teams to exhibit their exceptional sporting talents and compete for superiority!

    The first edition of BOSM was held way back in 1986 and has grown to be one of the largest and most competitive college sports fests in the country. Baxter, the desert eagle, is the official mascot of BOSM. A student-managed fest, BOSM 2021 is the 35th edition of this prestigious and exemplary carnival of sports.

    With a plethora of sporting contests to compete in, alongside exhibitions, talks, prof shows and various other sports related events, BOSM promises to be a thrilling four day experience full of adrenaline and fierce competition amongst the best in India.
